About

Hello! We are a team of 6 computer science students attending Washington State University. Our project, Customer Experience Management Tool (CXM) allows a selected company to read tweets directed towards their company and to classify them. For this project, we selected Epic Games as the company. The categories of classification are currently: positive, bugs and glitches, security/hackers, store, wants, and lastly, junk (to store unreadable tweets). Deriving from the Bag of Words model, and utilizing SciKitLearn, Joe and Elliott have developed a ML model. This project was created to show how customers will respond to certain company features and how we can train such data with a machine learning model.

How to use

  • Open the navigation menu at the top left.
  • Under the menu, hover over "Tweets" and select "Unclassified".
  • This is where we will classify our tweets and retrain the model.
